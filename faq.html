Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our process and ATS optimization.

What exactly is ATS optimization?

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are bots that scan your CV for keywords. We optimize your layout and content so these bots rank you higher, ensuring your CV actually reaches a human recruiter.

I’m switching industries entirely. Won't I look underqualified?

Not at all. We specialize in identifying transferable skills—taking your past successes and re-framing them to meet the requirements of your new target industry.

Is my personal information and career history safe with you?

Absolutely. We take data privacy seriously. Your documents and personal details are used strictly for the crafting process and are never shared with third parties or used for marketing without your explicit consent.

Do you write CVs for international job markets (UK, USA, Middle East)?

Yes. Different regions have different standards (such as CV vs. Resume formats). We stay updated on global hiring trends and can tailor your documents to meet the specific cultural and professional expectations of the country where you are applying.

Still have questions?

We're happy to help. Send us a message and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
