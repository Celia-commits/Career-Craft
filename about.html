About Us

Our Mission

We believe your past doesn't have to dictate your future. Career Craft was founded to bridge the gap between outdated narratives and your next big move."

Why Career Craft?

The job market is changing. With 75% of CVs being filtered out by automated systems (ATS), a great candidate can easily get lost in the digital void. We specialise in "translating" non-traditional backgrounds into the specific language recruiters are searching for.

Whether you are pivoting to a completely new industry or climbing the corporate ladder, we help you craft a narrative that proves you belong in the room.

How We Work

1. The Discovery

We analyze your background and target roles to find your unique "edge".

2. The Craft

Our experts rewrite your CV from scratch, optimizing for both ATS and human recruiters.

3. The Launch

You receive your polished documents, ready to land you that interview.
